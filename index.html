Dulce Amor

Welcome and thank you for visiting!

Dulce Amor, located in Toronto’s bustling Queen East area, offers a total of 28 cupcake flavours, with eleven available in-store every day. To give you a taste of our menu, try these tempting cupcakes: The "Yonge 'n' Eligible" cupcake featuring vanilla buttercream on a vanilla or chocolate cake; the chocolate buttercream on chocolate cake "James Brown"; and our marble "Frost Me" cupcake topped with vanilla frosting that changes colour seasonally.

Our cupcakes names are cleverly chosen by owner Cynthia Basilio and her staff of cupcake artists to make you smile. All our cupcakes are baked daily from the freshest ingredients. We also offer custom cupcakes, mini cupcakes, cake pops, gluten-free cupcakes and hand-illustrated cakes made to suit your special celebration. Dulce Amor is Toronto’s first and finest cupcake bakery, and we know how much fun a cupcake can be!
